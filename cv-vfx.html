About

I started as an artist — someone who believes that the most powerful thing a moving image can do is make you stop and just stare. Over time I became equally curious about how everything works underneath, and became the person who builds the tools others use — and spots why a workflow breaks three steps before anyone else notices.

I bring both: the eye for what looks right, and the instinct for why the system behind it doesn't scale. That combination is rare, and I think it's the point.

10+ years in CGI production, 4+ as a Senior VFX Artist, and the last two years moving deliberately into pipeline development and technical direction. I work across the full stack of VFX production — from lookdev and procedural effects to DCC integration, render farm orchestration and automation tooling. The creative and the technical aren't opposites for me — they inform each other constantly.
